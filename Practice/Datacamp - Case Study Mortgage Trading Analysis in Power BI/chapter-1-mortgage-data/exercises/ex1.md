##  Assignment 

The financial system is a network of borrowers, lenders, banks, and financial institutions that move money around in the economy. They all have an important role, and a healthy financial system is key to a great economy.

##  Instructions 

Match the role definitions with each participant in the financial system.



##  Hints 

Review the financial system diagram.

#### 1. People

Save in **Banks & Financial Institutions** to receive interest. Borrower from **Lenders** to get money.

#### 2. Banks &amp; Financial Institutions

Receive money from **People** to buy investments. Buy loans from **Lenders** as investments, and return interest to the **People** who saved with them.

#### 3. Lenders

Lend money to **People** in exchange for fees and a loan agreement. Sell loans to **Banks & Financial Institutions** to get money to keep lending to **People**.
