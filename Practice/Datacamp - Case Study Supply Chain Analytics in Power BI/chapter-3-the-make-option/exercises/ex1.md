
#  Incremental or sunk cost

```
Exercise ID 1558807
```

##  Assignment 

Let's practice identifying the difference between incremental and sunk costs. Incremental costs are only incurred if we move forward with a certain decision (make or buy), while sunk costs occur regardless of our decision.

##  Instructions 

For each activity, identify whether it is an incremental or sunk cost.



##  Hints 

A sunk cost exists regardless of our decision. It often is a pre-existing cost that was incurred by another decision. 
An incremental cost is a new cost resulting from our decision.
