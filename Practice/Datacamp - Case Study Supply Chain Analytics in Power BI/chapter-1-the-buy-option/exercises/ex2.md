You are hired as a supply chain analyst for Tenate Industries and your task is to create analyses that help the company determine make versus buy decisions for many products. The supply chain team has already requested quotes from your suppliers.

Time to explore the quote data in detail. 

##  Hints

For the **Y-axis**, select either `Supplier` or `Volume` from the `Quotes` table. Make sure the field summarizes by **Count** and not **Count (Distinct)**.
